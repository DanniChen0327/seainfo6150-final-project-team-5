Three words: attractive, clearly, warm

Attractive:
The goal of our team is to build a recipe website with beautiful pictures of dishes and brief introductions. The colorful pictures of dishes and clear classifications will be attractive to users. The website also has a recommendation of special dishes for traditional festivals, which is unique and makes the website stand out.

Warm:
The color of our website will be bright color and the pictures are bold and attractive, users may feel relax and enjoyable when browsing our website. Looking through the dishes will make users recall good memories and feel warm. The detailed dishes instructions is friendly to cooking novices, giving them courage to be brave to try out cooking and make them warm.

Clearly:
The classifications in the website is concise and clear, which makes users easily find out what they want. The contents about the recipes and description of dishes are detailed and articulate, users can easily get the information and successfully make the dishes by following the steps.
